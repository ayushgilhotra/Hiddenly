How to Use Hiddenly

1. Discovering Spots

Browse the Explore page to see a list of hidden gems shared by others. You can filter by category like Cafes, Nature, or Adventure to find exactly what you're looking for.

2. Finding the Location

Each spot has a secret location. Since these are "hidden" gems, the exact building might be hard to find! Click on any spot card in the Explore list, and we'll show you its exact location on the map at the bottom of the page.

3. Sharing Your Own Gems

Found a place nobody knows about? Login or Register to unlock the "Add Spot" feature. You can upload a photo, describe the vibe, and drop a pin on the map to help other explorers find it!
